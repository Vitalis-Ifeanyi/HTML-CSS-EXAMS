BIOGRAPHY.

From Particles to Pixels: My Unconventional Journey from Industrial Physics to Front-end Software Development.

Early Beginnings: Industrial Physics.

I started my academic journey in Industrial Physics, fascinated by the application of theoretical principles to real-world problems. Through internships, I gained hands-on experience in materials science, thermodynamics, and mechanical systems. Although I found the work intriguing, I soon realized that the field's limitations and isolated nature left me seeking more.

A New Spark: Discovery of Front-end Development.

My internship at Nupat Technologies marked a pivotal moment. Working on a data visualization project, I was introduced to HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Captivated by the creative possibilities and instant gratification of building from scratch, I began exploring Front-end Development. This encounter ignited a curiosity that would change my career trajectory.
